Financial Modelling

Creating a mathematical representation of the company finances


Essential tool to analyze the financial health of the company and for managers to determine informed business decisions


Involves creating financial statements, projecting future cash flows etc.

Financial Valuation

Determining the value of company/asset Helps us determine whether an investment opportunity is worth pursuing
